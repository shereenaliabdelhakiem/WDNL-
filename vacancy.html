Position description

The company-manufacturer of high-quality fences and other fences, invites to work welders MIG / MAG 135. 

Work performed:
welding of sheets with a thickness of 20-30 mm;
MIG welding of structural profiles and sheets of carbon steel;
welding according to technical drawing.

Requirements:
-understanding the Polish language
-experience minimum one year, ability to work with MIG / MAG semiautomatic;
-mindfulness and accuracy in the performance of work;
-desire to work.

Schedule: one shift of 10-12 hours per day, Monday to Friday at 06-17. Saturday, Sunday-weekend.
From 200 hours per month.

Payment: rate per hour is 28 PLN net. Monthly 4200-5550 PLN net. Accommodation provides a comfortable private house in Słupsk, in rooms 3-5 persons. Accommodation is free of charge, only the administrative fee + Utilities is paid per month: PLN 400 per month. For convenience, this amount is deducted from the salary. When checking in for an overnight stay, the candidate pays a deposit of PLN 200. Pets are not allowed.

Additional information:
We help to make medical book (cost 200 PLN deducted from salary).
