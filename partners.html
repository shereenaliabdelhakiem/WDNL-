You have the opportunity to become a representative of the Work Department NL

We are the only Polish company in Europe that closely cooperates with agencies from Asia and Africa.

We provide comprehensive services to companies in the field of personnel selection and employment of foreigners in our company in Poland.

By working with us, you and your customers
get a wide range of services:

  1. We send detailed instructions on how to prepare documents for submission to the visa application center and provide advisory support;
  2. Before submitting all documents to the visa application center, we send an official letter to the consul in order to obtain a positive result of the consideration of our clients ' cases, and if necessary, we receive calls from the consul in order to clarify the employment details;
  3. We meet our clients at the airport and direct them to the city where the client will work;
  4. We provide instructions for living in a new country;
  5. We open a bank account and help to get a PESEL and a residence permit;
  6. We provide full support in the workplace;
  7. Six months later, we submit the documents of our employees for a residence permit;
  8. If necessary, our employees are trained in a new profession and receive a European diploma, which is quoted in most countries of the world and is completely free of charge for our employees;
