Work Department NL is a reliable and responsible business partner

Work Department NL is an employment agency that provides temporary work, outsourcing and recruitment services. We help to take the first steps on a career path, as well as support our clients in recruiting employees.

Our recruiters are located in many countries of Asia and Africa

We are the only agency in Europe that has a representative office in Asia and Africa.

We provide comprehensive services to companies in the field of replenishment of personnel shortages. Our team of experts specializes in hiring foreigners, mainly from Asia and Africa.

We take any new cooperation seriously, so we carefully check the companies of our partners. We also analyze all documents of future employees and conduct tests of their qualifications.
